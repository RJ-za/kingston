Dispatch Services

Professional dispatch solutions for all types of trucks and fleets

Expert Dispatch Solutions

At KingSton Dispatching, we provide comprehensive dispatch services tailored to your specific fleet needs. Our experienced dispatchers work around the clock to ensure your trucks are always on the most efficient routes, carrying the most profitable loads, and meeting all delivery deadlines.

We understand the complexities of the transportation industry and offer solutions that maximize your fleet's productivity while minimizing operational costs.

Truck Types We Dispatch

Dry Van

Standard enclosed trailers for general freight. Our dispatchers specialize in finding the best dry van loads with minimal deadhead miles and competitive rates.

  • 53' Standard Trailers
  • High-Value Freight
  • LTL & Full Truckload

Refrigerated

Temperature-controlled transportation for perishable goods. We ensure your reefer loads are properly scheduled with appropriate temperature requirements and handling instructions.

  • Temperature Monitoring
  • Food-Grade Compliance
  • Pharmaceutical Transport

Flatbed

Open trailers for oversized or irregularly shaped cargo. Our dispatchers are experienced with securing permits, planning routes for oversized loads, and ensuring proper securement.

  • Oversized Load Routing
  • Permit Acquisition
  • Tarping Requirements

Heavy Haul

Specialized transportation for extremely heavy or oversized equipment. We handle all aspects of heavy haul logistics, from route planning to escort coordination.

  • Multi-Axle Configurations
  • Escort Coordination
  • Bridge Analysis

Intermodal

Container transportation between rail yards, ports, and destinations. Our dispatchers coordinate seamlessly with rail schedules and port operations to minimize delays.

  • Port Coordination
  • Rail Schedule Integration
  • Customs Documentation

Specialized

Custom solutions for unique cargo requirements. From hazardous materials to sensitive equipment, we have the expertise to handle your specialized freight needs.

  • Hazmat Certified
  • High-Security Shipments
  • White Glove Service

Our Dispatch Process

1

Load Sourcing

We search through thousands of loads daily across multiple load boards and direct shipper relationships to find the best matches for your equipment and preferences.

2

Rate Negotiation

Our experienced dispatchers negotiate the best possible rates for your loads, ensuring you maximize your revenue on every mile.

3

Driver Assignment

We match the right driver with the right load based on location, hours of service, equipment specifications, and driver preferences.

4

Documentation

We handle all necessary paperwork, from rate confirmations to delivery instructions, ensuring everything is properly documented.

5

Route Planning

Our team creates optimized routes that account for traffic patterns, construction, weather conditions, and hours of service regulations.

6

Real-time Tracking

We monitor your shipments in real-time, providing updates to customers and addressing any issues that arise during transit.

7

Delivery Confirmation

We confirm successful deliveries and collect necessary proof of delivery documentation to expedite the billing process.

8

Continuous Planning

While one load is in progress, we're already securing your next load to minimize empty miles and maximize your earning potential.
