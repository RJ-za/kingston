Billing Services

Comprehensive billing and financial management solutions for transportation companies

Streamlined Billing Solutions

KingSton Dispatching offers comprehensive billing services designed specifically for the transportation industry. Our team of financial experts handles all aspects of your billing cycle, from invoice creation to payment collection, allowing you to focus on your core business operations.

We understand the unique challenges of transportation billing, including detention charges, accessorial fees, and multi-stop deliveries. Our solutions ensure accurate, timely billing that improves your cash flow and reduces administrative burden.

Our Billing Services

Invoice Management

  • Invoice Creation

    We create detailed, professional invoices that include all relevant information required by shippers and brokers, ensuring faster payment processing.

  • Document Collection

    Our team collects and organizes all necessary documentation, including proof of delivery, bills of lading, and rate confirmations.

  • Quick Turnaround

    We process invoices within 24 hours of delivery completion to accelerate your payment cycle and improve cash flow.

  • Electronic Billing

    We offer electronic billing options compatible with major transportation management systems and EDI requirements.

Payment Processing

  • Payment Tracking

    We monitor all outstanding invoices and maintain detailed records of payment status to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

  • Factoring Coordination

    For clients using factoring services, we coordinate directly with your factoring company to ensure smooth processing and maximum approval rates.

  • Direct Deposit Setup

    We help establish direct deposit arrangements with brokers and shippers who offer this payment option.

  • Payment Verification

    We verify all payments received against outstanding invoices to ensure accuracy and completeness.

Collections & Dispute Resolution

  • Aging Reports

    We provide detailed aging reports that categorize outstanding invoices by time periods, allowing for prioritized collection efforts.

  • Payment Follow-up

    Our team conducts regular, professional follow-up on overdue invoices to ensure timely payment without damaging business relationships.

  • Dispute Management

    We handle billing disputes efficiently, gathering necessary evidence and documentation to resolve issues quickly.

  • Collections Assistance

    For severely delinquent accounts, we provide support in escalating collection efforts while maintaining professionalism.

Financial Reporting

  • Revenue Reports

    We generate comprehensive revenue reports broken down by customer, lane, driver, or time period to help you analyze business performance.

  • Cash Flow Forecasting

    Our financial experts provide cash flow projections based on outstanding invoices and payment histories to help with business planning.

  • Profitability Analysis

    We analyze the profitability of different customers, lanes, and service types to help you make informed business decisions.

  • Tax Documentation

    We prepare and organize financial records needed for tax filing, making your quarterly and annual tax preparation more efficient.
