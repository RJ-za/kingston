About Us

Learn more about KingSton Dispatching and our mission

Our Story

Founded in 2010, KingSton Dispatching began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, efficient, and professional dispatching services to transportation companies of all sizes.

What started as a small operation with just three dispatchers has grown into a comprehensive dispatching service with a team of over 50 professionals serving clients across North America. Our state-of-the-art dispatch center utilizes cutting-edge technology to optimize routes, track shipments, and provide real-time updates to our clients.

Our founder, James Kingston, recognized the need for a dispatching service that truly understood the challenges faced by transportation companies. With over 15 years of experience in the transportation industry, James built KingSton Dispatching on the principles of reliability, efficiency, and exceptional customer service. Today, we continue to uphold these values while embracing innovation to stay ahead in an ever-evolving industry.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional dispatching services that optimize operations, reduce costs, and improve efficiency for transportation companies, enabling them to focus on their core business while we handle the logistics.

Our Vision

To become the leading dispatching service in North America, known for our innovative solutions, reliability, and commitment to helping our clients succeed in an increasingly competitive transportation industry.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, innovation, and customer focus guide everything we do. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients based on trust, transparency, and mutual success.
