Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start dispatching for my company?

We can typically begin dispatching for your company within 24-48 hours after completing the onboarding process. The onboarding process includes setting up your account, gathering necessary information about your fleet, and establishing communication protocols.

What information do you need to start dispatching for my fleet?

We'll need basic information about your company, details about your vehicles (including MC/DOT numbers), driver information, preferred lanes or regions, and any specific requirements or preferences for loads. We'll provide a detailed checklist during the onboarding process.

How do your dispatchers communicate with drivers?

Our dispatchers can communicate with your drivers through various channels, including phone calls, text messages, email, or through your existing fleet management software. We adapt to your preferred communication methods to ensure smooth operations.

Can I switch between service packages as my needs change?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your service package as your business needs evolve. We recommend a minimum commitment of one month for each package to properly evaluate its effectiveness for your operations.
